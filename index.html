Loops Assignment

Demonstrates for, while, and do-while loops with sum and product calculations.

1️⃣
For Loop (1–18)
For loop — used when you know exactly how many times to repeat. for(start; condition; increment)
2️⃣
While Loop (1–27)
While loop — runs as long as the condition is true. Use when you don't know exactly how many times to repeat.
3️⃣
Do-While Loop (1–15)
Do-while loop — runs the code first, then checks the condition. Always executes at least once.
➕
Sum of Odd Numbers (2–21)
Sum of odd numbers between 2 and 21
Modulus ( % ) — gives the remainder of division. If k % 2 != 0 → it's odd!
✖️
Product of Even Numbers (3–17)
Product of even numbers between 3 and 17
Product starts at 1 — not 0, because anything × 0 = 0. If m % 2 == 0 → it's even!
